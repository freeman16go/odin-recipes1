Guacamole Dip

Ingredients

  • 4 Hass Avocados
  • 1 Tomato
  • 3/4 of an Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps For Making

  1. Use a dull knife to lightly cut the avocado in half. Be aware of the large seed in the center.
  2. Scoop out the flesh and mash it in a bowl.
  3. Dice tomato, onion, cilantro — throw it in.
  4. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
  5. Mix it all together — done!
