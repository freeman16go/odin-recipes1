Meat Sauce Pasta

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Pasta noodle
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Sundried tomato
  • Minced garlic
  • Diced onion
  • Basil
  • Spinach
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • Oregano
  • 1/2 lbs of Ground Beef

Steps To Cooking

  1. Start by boiling a pot of water for the pasta noodles over low heat.
  2. Add a dash of salt and a bit of olive oil. The water should start boiling within 20 to 30 minutes.
  3. In the meantime, prepare the sauce.
  4. In a separate saucepan, add 1/2 lb of ground beef and cook over low heat until golden brown.
  5. Add minced garlic, diced onion, and sun-dried tomatoes.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  7. Add spinach and cook until it softens. Lower the heat to a simmer.
  8. Stir in tomato paste.
  9. Add tomato sauce and mix well.
  10. Let the sauce continue simmering on low heat.
  11. Meanwhile, check the pasta to see if it has softened.
  12. Once done, drain the pasta.
  13. Add the pasta to the sauce and mix thoroughly.
  14. Serve and enjoy!
